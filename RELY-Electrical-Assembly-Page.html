Wire Harnessing

Built for Routing, Continuity, and Repeatability

RELY builds wire harnesses from simple jumpers to complex multi-circuit assemblies. Every harness is built to reduce misrouting, prevent stress points, support clean installation, and eliminate avoidable troubleshooting. Every harness is also 100% continuity tested before it leaves our facility.

Control Boxes & Modules

Install-Ready Electrical Assemblies

Control boxes and electromechanical assemblies need to arrive ready to install, not ready to diagnose. RELY builds these assemblies with consistent layouts, defined wire paths, secure connections, and clear labeling so your team is not troubleshooting wiring differences from one unit to the next.
