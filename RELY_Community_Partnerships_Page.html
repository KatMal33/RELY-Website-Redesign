Manufacturing does not have a standards problem. It has an access and workforce development problem.

RELY believes the best person for the job should get the job. What many manufacturers are missing is not accountability or operational standards. It is access to capable people who have not been given the opportunity, training structure, or operational support required to succeed in manufacturing environments.

We do not lower the bar. We build systems that allow more people to reach it.

A Workforce Model Built for Manufacturing Growth

You don't have to build the infrastructure internally to benefit from it

Not every manufacturer has the internal infrastructure, bandwidth, or operational structure to recruit, train, and scale non-traditional workforces internally. By partnering with RELY, manufacturers can support workforce expansion while leveraging systems, training structures, and operational models that have already been developed and proven within production environments. RELY helps manufacturers expand workforce capability without building the entire infrastructure internally.

Without Workforce Infrastructure
  • Limited labor scalability when programs ramp or volume swings
  • Inconsistent onboarding across hires, shifts, and programs
  • Internal operational strain absorbing training and certification work
  • Reduced flexibility to chase growth opportunities
With RELY
  • Structured workforce systems already operating at scale
  • Broader labor access through proven training pipelines
  • Production-ready support that integrates with your operation
  • More scalable operations without internal infrastructure build-out

RELY also opens its facility to other manufacturers interested in learning how non-traditional workforces can be deployed successfully — visual work instructions, training structures, production support systems, and integration approaches.
