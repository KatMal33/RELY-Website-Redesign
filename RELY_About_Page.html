History

Built in a period of manufacturing disruption.

2020

COVID Operational Transition

A customer hand-assembling packaging for Verizon credit cards needed to relocate production out of its own facility. RELY moved the program into RELY-controlled space — and the company's foundation was set.

Takeaway

RELY was built by stepping into operational problems manufacturers needed solved immediately.

2021

6,000 SQ FT Facility Launched

The original workforce-training facility evolved into a value-added operation: simple wire harnesses, light assembly for injection molders, kitting, rework, and secondary operations.

Takeaway

Customer demand accelerated as manufacturers looked for flexible operational support without internal complexity.

2022

2.5M–4M Parts Sorted Weekly

RELY scaled into large-volume sortation for a major injection molder, plus third-party sortation for Tier 1 automotive suppliers. Pre-trained, plug-and-play operator teams replaced the old playbook of pulling production operators or deploying untrained temps.

Takeaway

The objective was not labor support. The objective was operational control during high-risk manufacturing events.

2024

Expansion into FBA & HVAC Harnesses

Variety-pack assembly and Amazon FBA fulfillment for a beverage manufacturer. Prototype and service wire harness manufacturing for a major HVAC OEM. Both required higher responsiveness, process discipline, and scalability.

Takeaway

The more variable, disruptive, or execution-heavy the work, the more value RELY creates for customers.

2025

52,000 SQ FT Facility Launch

RELY relocated into a facility designed for multi-program execution across varied industries — engineered for high-volume sortation, automation integration, structured quality systems, and rapid program deployment.

Takeaway

The expansion reflected increasing customer demand for flexible, scalable operational manufacturing support.

What This Means For Customers

A partner built to absorb operational drag

When production volume grows, mix expands, and skilled labor gets stretched thin, manufacturers need a partner who can step in without adding complexity. RELY takes on the execution-heavy work — assembly, sortation, harnessing, fulfillment — so your team stays focused on the work that drives the business forward. The result is operational continuity that scales with growth, not against it.

Without RELY
  • Skilled operators and supervisors pulled into execution-heavy support work

  • High-mix and variable-demand work creating constant operational drag
  • Downstream quality and delivery risk that grows with complexity
With RELY
  • Core teams stay focused on core production
  • Flexible capacity that scales with launches, ramps, and seasonal swings

  • Operational continuity preserved as complexity and volume grow

RELY helps manufacturers scale without sacrificing operational focus — protecting core production while we absorb the work that pulls it off course.
